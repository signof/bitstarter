We live in a globally connected age, yet we tend to become disconnected from our neighborhood. You just read a tweet about all the cool stuff someone found at a garage sale in some place across an ocean, yet you missed the tweets announcing the yard-sale down the street. No more! What-Here-Now? delivers you all the posts from your neighborhood. Visiting another city, no idea what gets people around here excited? No more! What-Here-Now? finds trending links based on location. Whatever place you are interested in What-Here-Now? provides you with a local feed of all your favourite social sites.
